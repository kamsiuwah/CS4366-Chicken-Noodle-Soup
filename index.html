Welcome to Smart City Environmental Monitoring System

The Smart City Environmental Monitoring System is a web-based application that leverages IoT sensors to gather and analyze real-time data on air quality, noise levels, temperature, humidity, and pollution across the city. The system features an interactive dashboard for data visualization, custom alerts for abnormal conditions, and predictive analytics for forecasting environmental trends. Additionally, citizens can report environmental issues, contributing to a collaborative effort in ensuring urban environmental health. The platform is designed for scalability, security, and high performance.

Purpose

The Smart City Environmental Monitoring System has been developed to analyze environmental data such as air quality, noise levels, temperature, humidity, and pollution in real time. This system aims to help city planners and the citizens with issues related to monitoring environmental health and enable incitive prompt actions on raising concerns or taking up relevant actions over environmental issues.

Scope

The system will gather information through a distributed IoT sensor network and then a cloud-based processing and visualization through a webpage dashboard, followed by forecasting the trends by using the historical data. It will be a custom alerts and citizen issues reporting system.
